# **App Name**: ResumeCanvas

## Core Features:

- Resume Data Input: Allow users to input and manage their professional details, education, experience, and skills through a structured form or by pasting existing content.
- Template Application: Dynamically render the user's data into the selected resume template, mirroring the visual style of the provided HTML/CSS.
- PDF Export: Generate and allow downloading of the rendered resume as a high-quality PDF document, optimized for print.
- AI Content Suggester: Provide a tool to suggest relevant bullet points or descriptions for job experience based on job role and industry, helping users craft impactful statements.
- Mobile-Responsive Display: Ensure the resume template scales gracefully and remains readable across various screen sizes before PDF generation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, warm orange-brown (#C9864A). This hue, derived from earthy tones, evokes professionalism and warmth.
- Background color: A subtle, light beige (#EDE5DA), providing a soft, inviting canvas that harmonizes with the primary color, offering visual comfort.
- Accent color: A deep, rich red-orange (#B74610) selected to provide a dynamic contrast while remaining within a complementary warm palette, drawing attention to key information.
- Headlines and prominent text: 'Playfair Display' (serif), chosen for its elegant and artistic feel, bringing a touch of refined sophistication. Body text: 'DM Sans' (sans-serif), offering a clean, highly readable, and contemporary look. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Minimalist and functional iconography: Utilizing simple geometric shapes or clear, concise emoji-like graphics for section indicators, aligning with the clean aesthetic of the template.
- Clear two-column structure: A well-defined sidebar for contact, skills, and education, complementing a main content area for profile and experience, ensuring information hierarchy and ease of scanning.
- Subtle entry animation: A 'fadeUp' effect upon page load, giving a polished and engaging first impression without distracting from the content.
